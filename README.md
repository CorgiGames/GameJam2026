# Card Attack

A Deck-building and Tower Attack game developed in Unity. Players draft a deck of units, buffs, and sabotages using an in-game economy, and then deploy them strategically to destroy enemy castle across multiple rounds.

## Gameplay Mechanics

### 1. Draft Phase
* **Economy System:** Players start with a set amount of coins to purchase cards.
* **Shop & Reroll:** The shop offers a random selection of cards. Players can spend coins to reroll the shop offerings (cost increases per reroll).
* **Deck Management:** Purchased cards are added to the active deck. Players can view their full deck and detailed card stats.
* **Special Cards:** Includes unique effects like rolling Double Dice for extra coins or generating random cards.

### 2. Combat Phase
* **Card Deployment:** Cards have cooldowns and can be played during real-time combat.
* **Unit Spawning:** Deploy active units to navigate the path and engage targets.
* **Tactical Decoys:** Manually place Decoy units anywhere on the screen using the mouse to distract turrets. Decoys take decay damage over time.
* **Spells & Sabotages:** Cast cards to temporarily freeze turrets, reduce their range/fire rate, or instantly destroy them. Heal units or boost their movement speed.

### 3. Progression
* **Multi-Round System:** The game features multiple rounds. The deck drafted in Round 1 carries over to Round 2, allowing for progressive deck-building.

## Controls
* **Left Click:** Interact with UI, select cards, and purchase items.
* **Right Click:** View enlarged details of a card in the Draft phase.
